The product, Service Tag 2.0, required a design revamp and an enhanced user experience, along with the addition of new features. These requirements were communicated by the marketing and business analysts, and the new features had already been promised to customers for the upcoming version.

 

To gather user insights for the new features, I engaged with the Project Management team who had direct interactions with customers. Through discussions and collaboration, we aimed to incorporate valuable user insights into the design and development of the new features.

To gain a deeper understanding of the requirements from a user's perspective, I began documenting user stories. Recognizing that the app had various touchpoints for different users, I employed story mapping as a technique. Story mapping allowed me to visually map out the user's journey and identify the key interactions and touchpoints within the app.

 

By breaking down the user stories, I could gain insights into the user's needs, pain points, and desired outcomes at each stage of their interaction with the app. This approach helped me create a comprehensive view of the user's experience and facilitated better alignment between user requirements and the app's features and functionality.

 

To validate my understanding of user goals, common scenarios, and tasks, I developed task flows. Task flows allowed me to visualize the step-by-step process that users would undertake to accomplish their goals within the app. By mapping out the user's journey, I could identify any potential obstacles or challenges they might encounter along the way.

 

Task flows helped me gain insights into the user's decision-making process, interactions with the app's interface, and the overall flow of their tasks. By identifying pain points or areas of confusion, I could address them proactively and make necessary improvements to enhance the user experience.

 

Additionally, task flows provided a valuable tool for communication and collaboration with stakeholders. It allowed us to align on the user's goals and ensure that the app's design and functionality supported a seamless and intuitive user experience.
