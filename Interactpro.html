The current implementation of the Interact Pro app's registration flow is causing confusion and hindering proper registration for Installers, who use the app to commission connected lighting systems.

What are the current challenges we're facing?

  • Lack of preparation work before the mechanic arrives on location.
  • Inability for the mechanic to independently create a project.
  • Cumbersome and time-consuming registration process due to excessive information requirements.
  • Mechanics desire to start quickly without unnecessary delays.
How can we enhance the current registration flow and make it more accommodating to different working styles?
Rapidly compiled a research report capturing the genuine quotes, statements, and pain points expressed by the end-users. Conducted a thorough discussion of the report with the stakeholder team, and diligently documented the identified user stories.
User stories were meticulously crafted and subsequently, in collaboration with product management, the UX issues were prioritized. The enhancement for the registration flow was successfully included in the backlog, ensuring its consideration for future development.
